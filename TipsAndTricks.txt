Hello! Welcome to the tips and tricks page!


Tip #1: 
	After you type in a word and get a msgbox
	saying whether it's an adverb or not, you
	can press the spacebar instead of the OK
	button to close it.

Tip #2:
	Entering "killdata" will immeadiately
	close the program.

Tip #3:
	Enter "helptips" to bring this page up.

Tip #4:

	your easygui package.
